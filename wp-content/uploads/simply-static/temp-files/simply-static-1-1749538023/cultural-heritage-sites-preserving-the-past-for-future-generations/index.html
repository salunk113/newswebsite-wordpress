cultural heritage sites preserving the past for future generations

root7 hours ago6 hours ago01 mins

Cultural heritage represents the legacy of physical artifacts and intangible attributes passed down from generation to generation. It includes monuments, traditions, languages, festivals, art, and values that define the identity of a community or nation. Preserving cultural heritage is vital, as it fosters a sense of belonging, continuity, and pride, connecting people to their history and shaping their future. Whether tangible like historic buildings or intangible like oral stories and traditional music, cultural heritage enriches our understanding of humanity and promotes respect for cultural diversity.
